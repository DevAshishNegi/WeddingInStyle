I am a detail person, a lover of visual storytelling, and a knowledge-hungry researcher of whatever catches my interest. I’m also a DePaul University graduate with a degree in advertising. During my time at DePaul, I had the privilege of working on several campaigns for my courses that helped me hone my creative thinking. Outside of DePaul, I gathered a diverse collection of work experiences that cultivated my open-mindedness, creativity, and adaptability. I’m looking for opportunities to put my education and experience to use in an art direction role.

In addition to advertising, I’m also passionate about photography. Photography came into my life in the form of an old camera my aunt and uncle gave me back in high school. That camera got me out of the house and out into the world around me. It turned strangers into friends, trash into art, and boredom into a space to be filled with creativity. Photography has stayed in my life ever since as a creative outlet and a way of appreciating the overlooked in the world around me. I find inspiration in discarded objects, abandoned places, and the people I meet.
